I don't believe in specializing in one thing. I believe in going deep across many things and finding the patterns between them. Every domain below informs the others. I started a project called **52 Skills** in January 2025: learn 52 skills, one at a time. I made it 13 weeks before realizing that rigid weekly sprints don't work for my brain. The project didn't die. It evolved. The number is the goal. Not the timeline.

---

## The Domains

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="card">
    <h4>AI &amp; Knowledge Systems</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Core Domain</div>
    <p>Building a personal knowledge system that integrates analog notebooks, AI collaboration with Claude and Gemini, and an Obsidian digital vault. More on this in the Scriptorium section below.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Self-Hosted Infrastructure</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Core Domain</div>
    <p>A homelab called the Athenaeum: Proxmox hypervisor running virtual machines for network storage, Docker services, and monitoring. Eight named devices across the network, 40TB of storage, all connected through Tailscale. The second wall of sovereignty.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Photography &amp; Videography</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Core Domain</div>
    <p>Capturing moments that matter and telling stories through a lens. Shooting on a Lumix S5II full-frame with Sirui VP-1 cine primes and editing in DaVinci Resolve Studio. Photo and video work as both personal archive and creative expression.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Making &amp; Fabrication</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Core Domain</div>
    <p>Working with hands. 3D printing, parametric CAD, and soon Arduino and soldering. The maker trilogy: fabricate, electrify, fuse. Physical proof that thinking and making belong together.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Classical Drawing</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Active Study</div>
    <p>Learning to observe before learning to render. Cast drawing from the atelier tradition. The same progression artists have followed for centuries, because it works.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Philosophy &amp; Literature</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Ongoing</div>
    <p>350+ books collected across 50+ domains. Reading across Seneca, Cain, Tolle, Holiday, Carnegie, Isaacson, Sennett, and Borges. The library grows faster than I read, and that's the point.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Gaming &amp; Challenge Runs</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Creative Outlet</div>
    <p>OSRS and Minecraft as mediums for constraint-based storytelling. Self-imposed rules, long-form narratives, patience as content. The Vellumere channel, when it launches.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h4>Faith &amp; Theology</h4>
    <div class="card-type">Foundation</div>
    <p>Christian faith as a quiet foundation underneath everything else. Reading Scripture, attending church with Holly, and trying to figure out what it means to steward the gifts and curiosity I've been given.</p>
  </div>
</div>
